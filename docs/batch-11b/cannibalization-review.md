# Batch 11B Cannibalization Review

No raw GSC query-by-page export is available, so no redirect or merge is authorized. Findings are based on intent, titles, content, internal links, route inventory, and the supplied page-level estimates.

| Pair | Overlap | Primary | Supporting | Non-URL remediation |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Homepage vs service pages | Broad product terms; homepage is national/brand hub | Specific service for product query | Homepage for brand/broad discovery | Short homepage title, descriptive anchors to money pages, preserve service specificity |
| Province hub vs Product × Province | Local modifier and product lists | Province hub for broad “สินค้าไอที + province” | Product × Province for named product | Keep province title broad; product pages remain narrow; no merge |
| Computer vs desktop/custom/gaming/company | High topical overlap | `/บริการ/รับซื้อคอมพิวเตอร์` for broad computer intent | Specialized URLs for subtype intent | Add “ตั้งโต๊ะ” and maintain explicit chooser links; company page owns lot/company intent |
| RAM vs RAM province/component pages | RAM transactional overlap | `/บริการ/รับซื้อแรม` nationally | Province variants for local modifier | Retain canonical RAM breadth and supporting links; no redirect |
| iPad identification article vs iPad service | Shared product, different intent | Article for “เช็กรุ่น/Gen/Axxxx” | Service page for “รับซื้อ iPad” | Immediate answer plus contextual service CTA; distinct titles/H1 |

There is no confirmed case where Google ranks two scoped pages interchangeably for the same primary query. Recommendation: monitor a future raw GSC page/query export before proposing consolidation.
